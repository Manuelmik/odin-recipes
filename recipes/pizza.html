Pizza Recipe


Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients and toppings for pizza can vary widely, including meats, vegetables, herbs, and different types of cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced green bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup of sliced onions
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the marinara sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni, black olives, mushrooms, green bell peppers, and onions on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, and garlic powder over the toppings.
  8. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
