Lasagna Recipe


Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and various other ingredients.

Ingredients and variations of lasagna recipes may include meat, vegetables, different types of cheese, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
